FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a laboratory test appointment?

You can schedule an appointment through our online booking system, by calling our customer service line at +91-234-567-8900, or by visiting our facility directly. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability.

What are your sample collection procedures?

We offer both in-lab sample collection and home collection services. Our trained phlebotomists follow strict biosafety protocols. Fasting requirements vary by test type - you'll receive specific instructions when booking.

How long does it take to receive test results?

Most routine tests are available within 24-48 hours. Complex genetic and specialized tests may take 5-7 business days. Results are accessible securely through our patient portal and emailed to registered addresses.

Do you offer research collaboration opportunities?

Yes! We actively collaborate with academic institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and research organizations. Please use the contact form above selecting "Research Collaboration" and our research team will reach out to discuss potential partnerships.
