FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to get test results?

Most routine tests are completed within 24 hours. Complex genetic and specialized tests may take 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email notification as soon as your results are ready.

Do I need to fast before blood tests?

Fasting requirements depend on the specific test. For glucose and lipid panels, we recommend 8-12 hours of fasting. Water is allowed. Detailed preparation instructions are provided when you book your appointment.

Is home sample collection available?

Yes! We offer convenient home collection services for most tests. Our trained phlebotomists will visit your home at your preferred time. Additional charges may apply based on your location.

How accurate are your test results?

We maintain 99.9% accuracy across all tests. Our laboratory is ISO 15189 certified and follows strict quality control protocols. All equipment is regularly calibrated and maintained.

Can I access my previous test results?

Absolutely! All your test results are securely stored in your patient portal for 5 years. You can access, download, and share them anytime. We also provide trend analysis for recurring tests.
